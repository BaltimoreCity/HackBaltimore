Funding

Prizes & Incentives

So, the big question: how do we fund such a big hackathon? Like most large-scale events, funding will largely be provided by sponsors.

But since we also want this to be a community-centered and community-driven event, individuals will also be able to donate money to a specific project that they care about. This will increase the prize money and encourage more techies to become involved in that project.

By encouraging all Baltimoreans to get directly involved in the hackathon via donations, time commitments, or tech skills, Hack Baltimore will allow everyone – not just the tech community – to invest in building a better future for Charm City.
