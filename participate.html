Funding

Prizes & Incentives

So, the big question: how do we fund such a big hackathon? Like most large-scale events, funding will largely be provided by sponsors.

But we won’t stop there. We want this to be a community-centered and community-driven event, so individuals will also be able to donate money to a specific project that they care about. This will increase the prize money and encourage more techies to become involved in that project.

Anyone who submits a problem must have a vested interest in seeing the project through to completion, so all submitters will be required to champion their project and actually use the solution that gets built.

Because, while the Baltimore Hackathon itself may only last for a few days every year, our mission is to build sustainable solutions that have a lasting impact.
